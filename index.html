GitHub Club · SIT Hyderabad

The official GitHub Student Club of Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. A student-led initiative fostering open-source culture, collaboration, and real-world developer skills beyond the classroom.

Who We Are

We are developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts united by a passion for building, learning, and contributing to the open-source community. From beginners to advanced coders, everyone has a place here.

What We Do

Through hands-on workshops, competitive hackathons, and peer-driven learning sessions, we transform theoretical knowledge into practical skills. We believe learning happens best by building together.
